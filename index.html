Platform Rules

Terms and Conditions

1 Account Security

  • Each student account is limited to one registered device for security purposes.
  • Sharing login credentials with others is strictly prohibited.
  • Any unauthorized access attempts will result in immediate account suspension.
  • Device changes must be requested through the administration.

2 Content Usage

  • All course materials are for personal educational use only.
  • Downloading, recording, or redistributing content is forbidden.
  • Copyright violations will result in legal action and account termination.
  • Access to paid content requires active enrollment and payment.

3 Payment Policy

  • All fees must be paid before course access is granted.
  • Refunds are available within 7 days of payment if no content has been accessed.
  • Bank transfer details are provided in the student portal.
  • Payment receipts must be preserved for verification.

4 Code of Conduct

  • Maintain respectful communication with teachers and fellow students.
  • Complete quizzes and assessments independently unless specified.
  • Report any technical issues or concerns promptly.
  • Follow the scheduled timetable for live sessions.

5 E-Commerce Terms

  • Products purchased cannot be returned unless damaged or defective.
  • Shipping times vary by location (3-7 business days within Sri Lanka).
  • Order confirmation requires acceptance of these terms.
  • Disputes must be raised within 48 hours of delivery.

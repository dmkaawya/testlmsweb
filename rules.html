Payment & Refund Policy

All payments on Nova X Edu are processed in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR). The following payment policies apply:

  • Payment Methods: Bank transfers, online payments (where available). All payment methods are configured by the platform admin.
  • Payment Verification: All payments must be verified by admin or teacher before access is granted. Please allow up to 24 hours for verification.
  • Proof of Payment: Students must upload a valid payment receipt or provide a transaction ID when submitting payment.
  • Monthly Payments: Class access is granted on a monthly basis. Failure to pay monthly fees will result in access suspension at the end of the billing period.

📌 Refund Policy: Nova X Edu does not offer refunds once payment has been verified and access has been granted, except in cases of platform error or technical failure on our part. Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of payment.

  • Disputed Payments: Payment disputes must be raised through the support ticket system within 7 days of the payment date.
  • Fraudulent Payments: Submitting fake payment receipts is a serious violation and will result in permanent account ban and potential legal action.

Account Security & Device Limits

To protect the integrity of the platform and prevent unauthorized access, the following device and session policies apply:

  • Device Limit: Each student account is allowed access from a maximum number of devices as set by the teacher (default: 2 devices). Exceeding this limit will result in access denial from new devices.
  • Session Management: Active sessions are monitored. Suspicious login activity may result in automatic session termination.
  • Account Sharing: Sharing your account login details with others is strictly prohibited. If account sharing is detected, the account will be suspended immediately.
  • Password Security: Students and teachers are responsible for maintaining a strong, unique password. Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Lost Credentials: If you lose access to your account, use the password reset feature or contact support.

📌 Nova X Edu tracks login IP addresses and device fingerprints for security purposes. This data is used solely to protect your account and enforce device limits.
